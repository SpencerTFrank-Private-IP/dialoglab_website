## Install TestFlight

Download [Apple's TestFlight app](https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664) from the App Store if you do not already have it. TestFlight is Apple's official channel for beta apps.

## Accept your invite

You will receive a beta invite by email from Dialog Lab or through Apple's TestFlight system. Open the email on your iPhone and tap the invitation link. TestFlight will open and show Dialog Lab as an available beta.

<div class="beta-docs__callout beta-docs__callout--tip">
  <strong>Use the same email</strong>
  Sign in to Dialog Lab with the same email address you used when joining the beta waitlist. Your invite is tied to that address.
</div>

## Install Dialog Lab

In TestFlight, tap **Install** (or **Update** if you already have a previous build). Once installed, open Dialog Lab from your home screen or from within TestFlight.

## Enable notifications

When prompted, allow notifications. Dialog Lab uses them to alert you when a match is found — especially important if you leave the app while waiting for a partner.

<div class="beta-docs__callout beta-docs__callout--info">
  <strong>Notifications are critical</strong>
  This app only works if you come back and keep the conversation alive. Without notifications, you may miss a match or your partner's messages.
</div>

## After you install

<ol class="beta-docs__steps">
  <li>Open Dialog Lab and complete <a href="/beta/first-setup/">First Setup</a> if this is your first launch.</li>
  <li>Mark at least one topic in the Topics tab before starting a session.</li>
  <li>When a new beta build is available, TestFlight will notify you. Open the app after updating; you may need to re-enable notifications.</li>
</ol>

## Beta limitations

<div class="beta-docs__callout beta-docs__callout--warning">
  <strong>You are using a beta</strong>
  Dialog Lab is currently at version 0.5.0. Features may change, and you may encounter bugs. Use TestFlight's built-in feedback option or see <a href="/beta/get-support/">How to Get Support</a> if something goes wrong.
</div>
