## What is Dialog Lab?

Dialog Lab is a civil space to listen and be heard on contentious topics — a straightforward way to connect with people who hold different views on issues that matter.

You are paired one-on-one with someone who holds a different view on a topic you care about. An AI moderator stays in the chat to keep things civil.

## The loop in four steps

<ol class="beta-docs__steps">
  <li><strong>Mark your topics.</strong> In the Topics tab, thumbs up or down on issues you care about. Only marked topics can be chosen for a conversation.</li>
  <li><strong>Start a session.</strong> From the Convos tab, tap Start a Session. Dialog Lab searches for a partner with an opposing view on a shared topic.</li>
  <li><strong>Have a moderated chat.</strong> Share why you hold your view, respond to your partner, and let the AI moderator keep the conversation on track.</li>
  <li><strong>Reflect together.</strong> When the conversation ends, leave feedback on what worked, what did not, and whether your perspective shifted.</li>
</ol>

## Before you begin

<div class="beta-docs__callout beta-docs__callout--info">
  <strong>What you will need</strong>
  An iPhone, the email address you used to join the beta, and notification permission enabled so you do not miss a match while the app is in the background.
</div>
