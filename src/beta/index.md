## What is Dialog Lab?

Dialog Lab pairs you one-on-one with someone who holds a different view on a topic you care about. An AI moderator stays in the chat to keep the conversation civil.

## Install the beta

1. Install [Apple's TestFlight app](https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664) from the App Store.
2. Open your beta invite email on your iPhone and tap the invitation link.
3. In TestFlight, tap **Install**, then open Dialog Lab.
4. Allow notifications when prompted — you will need them to hear about matches.

Sign in with the **same email** you used to join the beta waitlist.

## First-time setup

On first launch, walk through onboarding (a few minutes, once):

1. Enter your email and activation code (email stays private).
2. Create a username, home state, and avatar (visible to partners).
3. Set matching preferences: Response Speed (Slow/Fast) and Convo Style (Casual/Debate).
4. Review the [Rules of Engagement](/beta/moderation/), enable notifications, and accept the terms.

When onboarding finishes, Dialog Lab opens on the **Topics** tab so you can mark stances right away.

You can change avatar, home state, and matching preferences later under **Profile**.

## Start a conversation

<ol class="beta-docs__steps">
  <li>
    <strong>Mark topics.</strong> In the Topics tab, thumbs up or down on issues you care about. You are only matched on topics you have marked.
    <a class="beta-docs__learn-more" href="/beta/picking-topics/#how-to-mark-a-stance">Learn more</a>
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Get matched.</strong> From the Convos tab, tap Get Matched. Dialog Lab searches for a partner with an opposing view.
    <a class="beta-docs__learn-more" href="/beta/starting-a-discussion/#get-matched">Learn more</a>
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Chat.</strong> Share why you hold your view, respond to your partner, and let the AI moderator keep things on track.
    <a class="beta-docs__learn-more" href="/beta/moderation/#why-there-is-an-ai-moderator">Learn more</a>
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Reflect.</strong> When the conversation ends, leave feedback on how it went and whether your viewpoint shifted.
    <a class="beta-docs__learn-more" href="/beta/ending-a-discussion/#post-conversation-review">Learn more</a>
  </li>
</ol>

<div class="beta-docs__callout beta-docs__callout--info">
  <strong>Matching can take a while</strong>
  During the beta, the user pool is small. Finding a partner may take multiple hours in slow periods — keep notifications on and leave matching running if you can.
</div>

## Troubleshooting

- **Invite link does not work** — Open the email on your iPhone (not a desktop browser) and make sure TestFlight is installed first.
- **Wrong email / cannot sign in** — Use the same address that received the beta invite.
- **No match notifications** — Enable notifications for Dialog Lab in iOS Settings. Without them, keep the app open while searching.
- **App updated in TestFlight** — Open Dialog Lab after updating; you may need to re-enable notifications.
- **Bugs or crashes** — Screenshot the problem, share it, and choose TestFlight Feedback — or see [How to Get Support](/beta/get-support/).
