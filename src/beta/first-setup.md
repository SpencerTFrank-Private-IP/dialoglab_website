## Overview

The first time you open Dialog Lab, you will walk through a short onboarding flow. It takes a few minutes and only happens once. You can update most settings later from the Profile tab.

## Step-by-step setup

<ol class="beta-docs__steps">
  <li>
    <strong>Create your account.</strong> Enter your email to get started. It will remain private — other users never see it.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Enter your activation code.</strong> Check your email for a one-time code and paste it into the app.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Build your profile.</strong> Choose a username and home state. This information is visible to all users and helps partners know a little about you.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Customize your avatar.</strong> Pick an icon and background color. Like your username, this is visible to conversation partners.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Set matching preferences.</strong> Choose your <em>Response Speed</em> (Slow or Fast) and <em>Convo Style</em> (Casual or Debate). This helps match you with the best available partner.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Review the Rules of Engagement.</strong> Five simple rules to keep things civil (### Lets talk about these rules of engagement). See the full list on the <a href="/beta/moderation/">Moderation</a> page.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Enable notifications.</strong> Dialog Lab needs to reach you when a match is found or your partner replies. 
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Accept the terms.</strong> Read through the EULA and Privacy Policy, then tap I Agree to finish onboarding.
  </li>
</ol>

## What is public vs private?

| Visible to partners | Private |
|---|---|
| Username | Email address |
| Home state | — |
| Avatar | — |

You can update your avatar, home state, and matching preferences anytime from **Profile → Account Preferences** or **Profile → Matching Preferences**.

<div class="beta-docs__callout beta-docs__callout--tip">
  <strong>Screenshot the rules</strong>
  During onboarding, the app suggests screenshotting the Rules of Engagement. They also appear in the <a href="/beta/moderation/">Moderation</a> guide.
</div>
